### **H2** ()
**Гидроген**, it’s an antioxidant; also helps repair mitochondria; intoxication

### **L-theanine** (200 - 1000 mg)
**L-теанин** (an amino acid that is contained in a various plants and mushrooms, significantly in a green tea; it should to reduce or remove the stress and give me some energy, calm and focus)
- it has almost **no side effects**;
- also can add a caffeine (50 - 150 mg) for more energy (1 - 2 proportion of caffeine and theanine, for example: 50 mg caffeine and 100 mg L-theanine)
- **intake for 1 month** and take **a pause for 1 month** or use before difficult tasks (exams, stressful situations or other), not use it on a regular basis !
